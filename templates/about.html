Understanding SJF-P.

The **Shortest Job First (Preemptive)** algorithm, also known as **Shortest Remaining Time First (SRTF)**, is a scheduling policy where the process with the smallest remaining execution time is chosen next.

KEY CHARACTERISTIC

If a new process arrives with a burst time less than the remaining time of the current process, the CPU is preempted and given to the new process.
